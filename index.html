Hello and Welcome!

Hi everyone!
We are so glad you could join us here and take the time to get to know us better!

EVCC was established in 1990 and have been going strong ever since. We are a non-profit organization, that was first planted by Evangelical Formosan Church of East Valley as a community outreach ministry.

We are a chorale group with ages that begin from 7 years old to 18 years old. Stop by on Sundays and see what we do!
